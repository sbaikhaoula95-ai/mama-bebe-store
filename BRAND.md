## 2. Identity — what we are

**Hnina is a modern Moroccan natural apothecary for mom & baby.**
A small, focused house of care that takes the trust of a Moroccan pharmacy, the gentleness of a mother, and the heritage of *beldi* ingredients (argan, prickly pear, calendula, rosemary, watercress) — and turns them into a tight line of safe, premium, daily-ritual products.

We are **not** a generic e-commerce brand. We are **the trusted Moroccan house** mothers come to when they want to be sure.

### Brand pillars (in this exact order)

1. **Authority — like a pharmacy.** Pediatricians, midwives, dermatological testing, lab-verified, batch-coded, traceable cooperatives. Every claim is structure-function and provable.
2. **Tenderness — like a mother.** Warm Darija voice, never shaming, never pushy, never "miracle" language. We meet Salma where she is.
3. **Heritage — purely Moroccan.** Argan from Souss cooperatives, prickly pear from Agadir, lavender from the Middle Atlas, calendula from Moroccan herboristerie. Made *here*, for *here*.
4. **Honesty — pharmacy-grade transparency.** Full INCI lists, zero parabens/sulfates/fragrance/alcohol, real reviews only, COD-only (no payment risk), Moroccan-IP only analytics.

### Voice

**Tender like a mother, rigorous like a pharmacist.**
Arabic/Darija-first. French for credibility cues. English only on product back-labels.
Never patronising, never panicked, never hype. Calm, specific, factual.

## 3. Positioning

> **For** every Moroccan mother (and gift-giver) who is afraid of harsh chemicals and tired of guessing what is safe for her body and her baby,
> **Hnina is** the trusted Moroccan natural apothecary for mom and baby,
> **because** every product is made from local heritage ingredients, validated by pediatricians and midwives, dermatologically tested, halal, and delivered home in 24–48h with cash on delivery.
> **Unlike** drugstore baby brands (imported, expensive, generic) or souk oils (cheap, untraceable, scary),
> **Hnina** speaks Darija, is built *for* the Moroccan mother, and lives by one rule: **"if it's not good enough for your baby, it's not in Hnina."**

### Category framing

- **Not:** a "skincare brand," a "baby store," a "natural cosmetics line."
- **We are:** a **maison de soin naturel** — a *natural apothecary* — for the **motherhood journey** (pregnancy → newborn → baby skin).

### ICP recap

- **Salma (primary):** anxious Moroccan mom 26–34, urban, mom-groups on FB/IG/TikTok, terrified of chemicals, trusts *beldi* + pharmacy + doctor + midwife.
- **Khadija:** experienced mom, proof + value driven, buys multi-packs, reorders.
- **Gift-giver:** grandma/aunt for Sbou3, newborn, Eid → packaging + prestige.

## 4. Color system — "Apothicaire Naturel"

The palette is intentionally **clinical-natural**: ivory and apothecary green carry the pharmacy authority; gold and soft blush carry the maternal warmth. Together they say *"safe enough for the pharmacy shelf, warm enough for a mother's hand."*

### Core palette (locked)

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Base / paper | `cream` | `#FBF7F1` | All page backgrounds, premium calm. |
| **Primary authority (NEW)** | `pharmacy` | `#1F4F3A` | The brand's #1 colour. Pharmacy/apothecary deep green. CTAs, logo lockup, authority chips, footer. |
| Primary soft | `forest` | `#4F6F52` | Hover states, secondary CTAs, dark sections. |
| Botanical accent | `sage` | `#7C9A82` | Backgrounds, leaves, light chips. |
| Authority ink | `ink` | `#22332A` | Body text, headings — doctor-grade readability. |
| Maternal warmth | `blush` | `#D8A39B` | Mom/baby accents, ribbons, soft callouts. |
| Premium accent | `gold` | `#C7A35B` | "Liquid gold" prickly pear cues, badges, awards, ratings. |
| Alert | `danger` | `#B42318` | Form errors, phone-pickup callouts only. |

### Usage rules (strict)

1. **`pharmacy` (`#1F4F3A`) is the new primary CTA colour** — not `forest`. It is the "doctor-trusted" green. Use it on:
   - Header brand mark + footer brand wordmark
   - Main buy buttons ("اشتري الآن", "تأكيد الطلب", "تسوقي")
   - Authority chips ("ينصح به أطباء الأطفال", "مختبر جلديا")
   - Section dividers / pharmacy-cross visual motifs
2. **`cream` + `pharmacy` carry ~80% of the page.** Blush + gold are **accents only** (badges, ribbons, ratings) — never primary text.
3. **One bold CTA per screen.** Buttons in `pharmacy`. Secondary actions in outlined `pharmacy` border.
4. **Generous white space + clear hierarchy** — feels like a pharmacy display, not a bazaar.
5. **Trust chips use a consistent style:** ivory bg, `pharmacy` text, thin `sage/30` border.
6. **Pharmacy-cross visual motif (➕) is allowed** as a subtle decorative element on authority/section dividers — *never* gimmicky.

### What to avoid

- ❌ Cliché baby pink/blue.
- ❌ Pharmacy white-coat sterility (we are *natural* apothecary, not hospital).
- ❌ More than 2 accent colours visible in the same hero.
- ❌ Gradient text on body copy (only on hero headlines, sparingly).
