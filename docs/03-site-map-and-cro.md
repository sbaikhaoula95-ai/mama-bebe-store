# Site Map And CRO Specification

## Global CRO Principles

- Mobile-first. Most traffic will come from Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok mobile.
- Arabic-first and RTL.
- Every page must answer: "Is this safe? Is this trusted? What do I do now?"
- Repeat COD reassurance everywhere.
- Use real-looking DTC layout: spacious sections, strong typography, badges, reviews, ingredient proof.
- Images can be placeholders now, but every image slot must be named clearly for later replacement.
- CTAs should add selected offer to cart and open the cart drawer.
- No cart page.

## Product Page Structure

Each product page must act as a landing page.

### 1. Product Hero

Desktop: copy right, image left. Mobile: copy first.

Required:

- full Arabic product name
- outcome-focused headline
- short subheading
- star rating
- trust badges
- image placeholder
- offer selector (1/2/3 pieces)
- CTA: أضيفي للسلة
- COD reassurance
