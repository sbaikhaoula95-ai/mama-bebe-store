# Brand Positioning And ICP

## Brand Core

**Hnina / حنينة** means tenderness. The brand should feel like:

- a tender Moroccan mother,
- a serious pediatrician,
- a premium natural pharmacy brand,
- a clean DTC beauty brand.

The store must never feel like a cheap AliExpress landing page.

## Positioning Statement

**حنينة** is the premium Moroccan mom and baby care brand made from trusted natural ingredients, designed for the sensitive moments of pregnancy, bedtime, and baby skin.

Shorter version:

> حنينة: عناية طبيعية وموثوقة لماما والبيبي، من مكونات كنثقو فيها فالمغرب.

## Category To Own

Own this category in the customer's mind:

**"The trusted Moroccan natural care brand for mom and baby."**

Not:

- generic cosmetics,
- random baby products,
- cheap dropshipping,
- medical treatment.

## Brand Promise

> Ila ma kanch mzyan 3la wlidek, ma kaynch f Hnina.

Arabic display:

> إلا ما كانش مزيان لوليدك، ما كاينش فحنينة.

Use this in About, footer, trust sections, and product pages.

## ICP 1: Salma

**Profile:** Moroccan mom, 26-34, pregnant or new mother, urban/semi-urban, uses Facebook/Instagram/TikTok, buys COD, follows mom pages.

**Deep fears:**

- "I don't want to harm my baby."
- "My body changed after pregnancy."
- "My baby is uncomfortable and I feel guilty."
- "I don't trust online stores."
- "If I pay cash on delivery, at least I feel safe."

**Trust triggers:**

- Pediatrician/midwife authority.
- Real Moroccan mom reviews.
- Natural ingredients she recognizes: argan, prickly pear, lavender, chamomile, calendula.
- Halal / dermatologically tested / lab-verified badges.
- COD and fast delivery.
- Clear address and contact page.

**Objections:**

- واش مامون على البيبي؟
- واش فعلا طبيعي؟
- واش غادي يجيب نتيجة؟
- واش نقدر نخلص حتى توصلني؟
- واش هاد الموقع موثوق؟
- شحال ديال الوقت باش يوصل؟

## ICP 2: Khadija

**Profile:** Experienced mom, already has 2+ children, practical, compares prices, buys bundles if value is clear.

**Trust triggers:**

- Bigger packs and value offers.
- Product reviews from moms like her.
- Clear usage instructions.
- Guarantees and easy phone confirmation.

## ICP 3: Gift Buyer

**Profile:** aunt, grandma, friend, husband, coworker buying for pregnancy, birth, Sbou3, Eid.

**Trust triggers:**

- Beautiful packaging.
- "هدية لماما والبيبي"
- Bundle offer.
- Delivery speed.

## Emotional Selling Pillars

### 1. Tenderness

Use words like:

- حنان
- لمسة حنينة
- عناية دافئة
- لحظة بينك وبين وليدك

### 2. Safety

Use words like:

- مامون
- لطيف
- مناسب للبشرة الحساسة
- بدون بارابين
- بدون كحول
- مختبر جلديا

### 3. Authority

Use:

- developed with pediatrician/midwife guidance
- dermatologically tested
- lab-verified
- ingredient transparency
- real batch and supplier proof when available

Do not fake authority. If not yet real, use placeholders in design and comments like `[replace with real credential]`.

### 4. Moroccan Pride

Use:

- مكونات مغربية
- زيت الهندية من سوس
- زيت الأرغان من تعاونيات مغربية
- علامة مغربية لماما والبيبي

### 5. Relief

Use:

- finally a simple ritual
- no complicated routine
- pay when received
- we call to confirm

## Brand Voice

Arabic-first, simple, direct, local, warm.

Avoid:

- over-medical language,
- shame,
- fearmongering,
- exaggerated claims,
- too much French,
- fake luxury language.

Good style:

> عارفة شحال كتقلقي من أي حاجة كتستعمليها فالدار مع البيبي. لذلك حنينة كتختار مكونات طبيعية، لطيفة، ومفهومة.

Bad style:

> This revolutionary treatment eliminates stretch marks and cures eczema fast.

## Trust Stack To Display Everywhere

Use these badges/components:

- الدفع عند الاستلام
- توصيل 24-48 ساعة
- مختبر جلديا
- مناسب للبشرة الحساسة
- مكونات طبيعية
- بدون بارابين
- بدون كحول
- صنع/عبأ في المغرب
- ضمان حنينة

## Authority Assets To Prepare

The website can include placeholders, but production should replace with real proof:

- Pediatrician quote + name + photo.
- Midwife quote + name + photo.
- Lab test image or certificate.
- Supplier origin photo/video.
- UGC videos from Moroccan moms.
- Review screenshots.
- Packaging photos.
- Ingredient close-ups.
